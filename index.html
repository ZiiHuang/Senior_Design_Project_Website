How It Works

A simple process: define the logic once, then let the system sort with speed and precision.

  1. Identify: The AI vision system scans incoming items by material, shape, or custom labels.
  2. Command: Your custom app logic determines the destination for every object.
  3. Execute: The IoT-enabled conveyor routes each item to its designated bin with high accuracy.
  4. Analyze: Monitor throughput and bin capacity in real time through the dashboard.
